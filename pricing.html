Pricing FAQ

Is Spokable PDF free?

Yes, the application itself is completely free and open source. You only pay for Google Gemini API usage based on your consumption.

How do I get started for free?

Visit Google AI Studio and create a free API key. The free tier includes generous limits suitable for personal use.

When do I need to pay?

You only need to upgrade to a paid plan if you exceed the free tier limits (15 requests/minute or 1,500 requests/day). For most users, the free tier is sufficient.

How can I track my usage?

Visit the Google AI Studio dashboard to monitor your API usage and costs in real-time.
