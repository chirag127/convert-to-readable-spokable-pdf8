Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

Spokable PDF ("we", "our", or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our PDF conversion service.

Data Collection

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your PDF files to any server
  • We do NOT track your browsing behavior
  • We do NOT use cookies or analytics
  • We do NOT require account creation or personal information
  • We do NOT sell or share any data with third parties

What Stays on Your Device

  • API Keys: Stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage
  • Settings: Your preferences (model selection, prompts, etc.) stored in LocalStorage
  • PDF Files: Temporarily stored in IndexedDB during processing, automatically cleared after completion
  • Dark Mode Preference: Stored in LocalStorage

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API

When you process a PDF, extracted text is sent to Google's Gemini API for transformation. This is governed by Google's privacy policy:

  • Only the extracted text content is sent (not the entire PDF file)
  • Your API key authenticates the requests
  • Google's data usage policies apply to API interactions
  • Review Google's Gemini API Terms

CDN Resources

The application loads PDF.js and jsPDF libraries from CDNs. These are standard, widely-used libraries with no tracking or data collection.

Data Security

  • All processing happens client-side in your browser
  • API keys are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • No backend database or server-side storage exists
  • Your files never leave your device except for text sent to Gemini API

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View your settings and API keys in the Settings page
  • Delete: Clear your browser's LocalStorage and IndexedDB to remove all data
  • Export: Your settings are stored in plain text in LocalStorage

Children's Privacy

Spokable PDF does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The service is designed for general use and does not require age verification.

Open Source

Spokable PDF is open source. You can audit the entire codebase on GitHub to verify our privacy claims.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
